# Business Cases

## Smart Factory Robot Fleet Monitoring

Manufacturers need reliable autonomous movement and manipulation inside facilities. A Physical AI system can combine robot telemetry, edge inference, SLAM quality, and network latency to predict operational risk before downtime occurs.

## Warehouse AMR Navigation

Autonomous mobile robots rely on localization quality, low latency, and obstacle handling. Simulation and digital twins make it possible to test navigation policies before exposing real hardware to risk.

## Robotic Arm Inspection and Sorting

Industrial arms can use vision and learned policies for inspection, sorting, and pick-place tasks. Sim-to-real workflows reduce data collection cost and improve repeatability.

## AI-RAN Edge Robotics

Private 5G and AI-RAN architectures can prioritize latency-sensitive robotics traffic, place workloads intelligently, and forecast congestion that could degrade robot safety or throughput.
